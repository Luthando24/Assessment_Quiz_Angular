Assessment instructions

The assessment has three sets of multiple choice questions you can choose from and the three sets of questions are programming languages namely JavaScript, Asp.net and general Design patterns, you are advised to select the programming language you are most comfortable in for better results, please also note that upon selection of the test you prefer to take the test will resume immediately so only select the option when ready to start and not prior preparations. Each test is consistent of 10 questions timed at 10 minutes giving you a maximum of 1 minute per question to complete.
